me.

Born in Louisville, raised around all things Kentucky, I took to the arts at an early age. This has developed into a career as a designer and new front-end web developer.

I graduated from the University of Louisville in 2006 with a double major in Communication Art & Design and Art History. Since graduating I have gained experience in all areas of print design. I am beginning to tap into the wide world of web and digital.

I am currently a freelance designer, front-end developer in training, mom, friend and board member of AIGA Louisville.
